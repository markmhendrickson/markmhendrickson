## LinkedIn

I expected customer research calls to give me the clearest signal on Neotoma.

They didn't.

Some of the sharpest feedback came from asking people to have their own AI agents evaluate the product against their real workflows. The agents were more direct about disqualification, more specific about use cases, and better at mapping the product to concrete pain points in the person's stack.

That process changed more than my positioning. It changed the acquisition flow itself. The site now asks agents to evaluate before humans install.

I wrote up what worked, what didn't, and why agent-mediated evaluation may become a real channel for technical products.
