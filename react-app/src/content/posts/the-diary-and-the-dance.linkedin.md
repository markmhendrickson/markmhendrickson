I recorded a voice memo this morning about a habit I want to cultivate more deliberately: continually revealing my inner world to the external one, and tuning into what comes back.

It led me to reconsider the lean startup model. The MVP isn't a static point you ship and wait for a verdict on. It's a continuum. Every version is an MVP. The feedback is never binary. And the whole process is more art than science, especially at the start.

Same thing with writing. I used to treat it as a formal event. Now I think it needs to be embedded in daily life, like any other creative act. No right moment. No required audience. Just the practice.
