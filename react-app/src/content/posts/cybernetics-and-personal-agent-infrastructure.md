## Working thesis

Cybernetics (control and communication in the animal and the machine) is not a metaphor for agentic personal infrastructure—it is the intellectual lineage. A three-layer stack (truth / strategy / execution), feedback into rules and skills, and a durable memory substrate are the same structural commitments as Wiener, Ashby, and Beer made explicit for machines and organizations.

## Possible outline

1. **Steering**: *Kybernetes*—why “governor” and “steersman” name the problem (read, reason, act, observe).
2. **Feedback and self-regulation**: `/learn`, behavioral self-adaptation, retrospective instruction improvement—circular causality.
3. **Beer’s VSM**: Map System 5/4 → strategy, 3/3* → truth (Neotoma: audit, provenance), 1/2 → execution (scripts, MCP).
4. **Ashby’s law**: MCPs and scripts as variety amplifiers; rules and skills as constraints—only variety absorbs variety.
5. **Homeostasis**: Local-first, autonomous execution within bounds, escalation only for irreversible or high-risk actions.
6. **Second-order cybernetics**: The system observes itself (constraints, `/learn`, validation)—observer included in the loop.
7. **Neotoma as nervous system**: Without fidelity in observation and memory, feedback loops degrade; entity resolution and event sourcing as signal integrity over time.
8. **Closing**: What this framing buys—design vocabulary (where variety is unmatched), diagnostics (sense vs. model vs. act vs. learn), positioning for anyone who already knows Beer or Wiener.

## Raw material (from chat)

The core parallel is control and communication: agents that read structured memory, reason from strategy, execute actions, and adjust behavior from outcomes are cybernetic systems by definition.

**Feedback**: Interventions → durable rules/skills/hooks is textbook corrective feedback.

**VSM mapping**:

| Beer (VSM) | Layer | Role |
|------------|--------|------|
| 5 / 4 | Strategy | Identity, intelligence, principles |
| 3 / 3* | Truth | Coordination, audit—Neotoma as monitoring substrate |
| 1 / 2 | Execution | Operations, dampening oscillation—scripts, workers, MCP |

**Ashby**: Expand repertoire (MCPs, scripts, skills) to match environmental variety; use rules to attenuate where needed.

**Homeostasis**: Self-regulation inside bounds; escalate when perturbation exceeds autonomous capacity.

**Second-order**: System modifies its own controlling—retrospective improvement, agent constraints, validation loops.

**Neotoma**: Information channels need fidelity; without reliable memory, you cannot correct what you cannot observe or remember.

**Utility of the frame**: Variety gaps for roadmap; failure taxonomy (sensing vs. modeling vs. actuation vs. feedback); one-line pitch for readers who know Beer/Wiener—truth layer as the information substrate for a viable personal or organizational agent.
