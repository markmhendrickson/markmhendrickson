## 🧭 How to get here

Our home is located in the residential neighborhood of Gràcia on a quiet side street near the center of Barcelona, just minutes walking from the famous [Sagrada Familia](https://sagradafamilia.org/).

If you arrive by plane, you can take a 20–30 minute taxi from the airport for an estimated €35–40.

If you arrive by car, there are various parking garages in the vicinity, though they're relatively expensive.
