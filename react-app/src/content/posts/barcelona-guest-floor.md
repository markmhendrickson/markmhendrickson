## How to get here

Our home is located in the residential neighborhood of Gràcia on a quiet side street near the center of Barcelona, just minutes walking from the famous [Sagrada Familia](https://sagradafamilia.org/).

If you arrive by plane, you can take a 20–30 minute taxi from the airport for an estimated €35–40.

If you arrive by car, there are various parking garages in the vicinity.

## What's nearby

There are many sites to see within walking distance, with others conveniently accessible by bike, taxi or subway:

- [Sagrada Familia](https://sagradafamilia.org/) (5 minute walk)
- [Parc Güell](https://parkguell.barcelona/) (10 minute taxi or 27 minute walk)
- Central Gràcia (12 minute walk)
- Local farmers market (1 minute walk, open daily apart from Sunday)
- Supermarkets Veritas (all organic) and others, 2 minute walk
- Convenience store (1 minute walk)
- Wide boulevard of Sant Joan with kids playgrounds (2 minutes walk)
- Café with specialty coffee Bar La Camila (6 minute walk)
- Bakeries Origo and Turris (6 minute walk)
- Beach (15 minute taxi, 14 minute bike or 47 minute walk)
- Central Park / Ciutadella (7 minute bike or 30 minute walk)
- Gothic quarter / El Born / old city (8 minute bike or 27 minute walk)
- Las Ramblas (10 minute taxi, 10 minute bike or 33 minute walk)
- Passeig de Gràcia / luxury shopping district (6 minute bike or 15 minute walk)
- Montjuïc / magic fountain (15 minute taxi or 27 minute subway)

See [Spain visitor information](https://www.spain.info/en/) for restaurants and other recommendations.
