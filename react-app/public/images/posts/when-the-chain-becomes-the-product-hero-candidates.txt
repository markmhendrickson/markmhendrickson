# Hero image candidates: When the chain becomes the product

All candidates follow the mandatory style: solid black background, white line-art only (thin outlines, no fills or shading), elements in lower portion of frame, generous negative space above, no text. Stylized, iconic, minimalist.

---

## Candidate 1: Chain consuming the product

A small product shape (simple box or app-window silhouette) in the lower third, with chain links or block segments wrapping around it or overtaking it—the chain engulfing the product. Black background only. White line-art only, thin clean outlines, no fills or shading. Minimalist, iconic, stylized. No text or labels. Composition with elements in lower portion of frame and generous negative space above.

---

## Candidate 2: Receding road to horizon

A single path or road receding toward the horizon in the lower third of the frame; a small milestone marker (e.g. flag or stake) at the far end, suggesting the "moment of value" always moving away. Black background only. White line-art only, thin clean outlines, no fills or shading. Minimalist, iconic, stylized. No text or labels. Composition with elements in lower portion of frame and generous negative space above.

---

## Candidate 3: Token and diverted path

A coin or token (simple circle with minimal inner line) dominant in the lower third; a path or road that branches or bends toward the token while a smaller product shape (box or icon) sits on a fading or secondary path. Black background only. White line-art only, thin clean outlines, no fills or shading. Minimalist, iconic, stylized. No text or labels. Composition with elements in lower portion of frame and generous negative space above.

---

## Candidate 4: Narrative loop eclipsing product

Two concentric or overlapping loops in the lower third: a small tight loop (product feedback) and a larger loop (narrative) that surrounds or eclipses it. Or a single spiral that widens as it recedes. Black background only. White line-art only, thin clean outlines, no fills or shading. Minimalist, iconic, stylized. No text or labels. Composition with elements in lower portion of frame and generous negative space above.
